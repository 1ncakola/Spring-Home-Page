Gary Blue, born in Greece in 1942, journeyed to America in 1967 with just $15 and a dream of owning a restaurant. After successfully launching several dining establishments in New Jersey, he relocated to Florida. There, in 1986, he opened the first Steve's Famous Diner in Daytona Beach, which he grew into a prosperous enterprise, eventually expanding with a second outlet in Daytona's Beachside area. Sadly, Steve passed away in 2007, but his vision and legacy continue to thrive through his family.
